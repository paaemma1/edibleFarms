About Edible Farms

Born from a passion for clean, honest food, Edible Farms is a fresh Ghanaian startup on a mission: to bring premium, responsibly raised catfish to every table. We believe great taste shouldn't come at the expense of your health or the planet.

Our journey began in Ablekuma, Accra, where we started small but dream big. Today, we're proud to partner with local restaurants and families who share our vision for sustainable, delicious seafood. No shortcuts. No mystery. Just pure, fresh catfish.
