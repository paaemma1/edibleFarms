Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our catfish, ordering process, and delivery.

🐟 How is your catfish raised?

Our catfish are raised in pristine, natural ponds using sustainable aquaculture practices. We use NO antibiotics, NO growth hormones, and NO harmful chemicals. Our fish are fed a natural, plant-based diet and raised with care from fingerling to harvest.

📦 How fresh is your catfish?

Our catfish is harvested within 12-24 hours of delivery. We use refrigerated vehicles to maintain optimal temperature during transport. When you receive your order, the fish is still fresh, never frozen (unless you specifically request frozen).

🚚 Do you deliver outside Accra?

Yes! We deliver to Tema, Kumasi, Takoradi, Cape Coast, and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on location. For cities outside our standard delivery zones, please contact us directly to discuss options.

💰 What are your bulk order discounts?

Our bulk pricing is tiered based on quantity:

  • 25-50 kg: Standard pricing
  • 50-100 kg: 5% discount
  • 100-250 kg: 10% discount
  • 250-500 kg: 15% discount
  • 500+ kg: Custom pricing (contact us)

Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly contracts also qualify for additional discounts.

🌱 Is your catfish organic/sustainable?

While we are in the process of obtaining full organic certification, our farming practices exceed conventional standards. We follow sustainable aquaculture principles, maintain water quality, and prioritize environmental stewardship. No antibiotics, no hormones, no overcrowding.

🔪 How should I store the catfish?

Store fresh catfish in the refrigerator at 0-4°C and consume within 2-3 days for best quality. For longer storage, freeze immediately - our catfish freezes well for up to 3 months. Always thaw frozen catfish in the refrigerator overnight before cooking.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash on Delivery (for orders under GHS 500)
  • For bulk/contract customers: 30-day invoice terms available
🔄 Can I return or exchange my order?

If you're not satisfied with the quality of your catfish, contact us within 2 hours of delivery. We will either replace the fish or issue a full refund. Please see our Shipping Policy for complete details.

🏢 Do you offer restaurant/wholesale contracts?

Absolutely! We specialize in providing reliable supply to restaurants, hotels, and catering businesses. We offer:

  • Flexible delivery schedules (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
  • Tiered volume pricing
  • Invoice payment terms for established customers
  • Custom processing options

Visit our Bulk Orders page to request a quote.
